About Us

BINI is a premier Filipino pop (P-pop) girl group managed by ABS-CBN under Star Magic and Star Music. Officially debuting in June 2021, BINI is composed of eight talented members: Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Sheena, and Jhoanna. Renowned for their exceptional synchronization in dance and captivating pop melodies, BINI stands at the forefront of the P-pop industry. Their music seamlessly blends Filipino and English lyrics, often highlighting themes of empowerment, self-confidence, and national pride. With a mission to inspire and uplift the younger generation, BINI continues to carve their path in the global music scene, amassing a dedicated fanbase known as "Bloom."
